Our Graduates

Hermione Granger

Hermione excelled in every subject at Hogwarts and went on to make significant contributions to the wizarding world as a leader in the Department of Magical Law Enforcement.

Cedric Diggory

A highly talented wizard, Cedric represented Hogwarts in the Triwizard Tournament and is remembered for his courage and fair play.

Luna Lovegood

Luna became a renowned wizarding naturalist and is well known for her unique perspective and kindness, always willing to help her peers.

Harry Potter

The Boy Who Lived, Harry became a renowned Auror, defeating Voldemort and later leading the fight against dark forces in the wizarding world.

Ron Weasley

Alongside Harry and Hermione, Ron played a vital role in the defeat of Voldemort and later co-founded Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes, a successful joke shop.

Draco Malfoy

Draco redefined his family's legacy, turning away from dark influences and contributing to magical advancements in potion-making and magical law.

Neville Longbottom

Neville overcame his struggles and became a celebrated Herbology professor at Hogwarts, inspiring countless students.

Molly Weasley

Molly is known for her immense love for her family and her pivotal role in the Battle of Hogwarts, where she bravely fought to protect her loved ones.
